### Fibula Type
‘Bent-stem’
### Description
This fibula is fairly corroded, and appears to have had something covering part of the foot due to a less corrosive patch. It has a normal spring system.
### Culture
Roman
### Period
 Roman Imperial
### Date
Early 2nd Century CE
### Material
 Bronze
